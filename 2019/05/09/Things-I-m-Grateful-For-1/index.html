#1 Things I'm Grateful For

Posted on 2019-05-09

$\color{red}{I’m happy today.}$

$\color{red}{Weather is good.}$

I’m always happy when the weather is fine, and sad when it’s not.

$\color{red}{I answered some question voluntarily on the Problem Solving class.}$

I don’t usually do that. In fact, few people do that. However, I did it today.

I have a sweet tooth, so that I bought myself a cup of milk tea as a reward. I had noodles for dinner. And it tasted good.

$\color{red}{Why do I write log like a child?}$

Write spetacular words needs time and energy. I’m quite reluctant to consume my mind. So this log may read like a child’s work.
